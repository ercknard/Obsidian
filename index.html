CRYSTALCRYPTS

CRYSTALCRYPTS


CrystalCrypts is a server plugin which changes Minecraft's play-style via server side modifications to the game. Players may craft additional items and materials out of resources from the world. These additional components then are used for crafting and constructing multi-block machine-like buildings. The constructed structures are operable by players, have a hit point system and can be destroyed by other players.

Players may craft structures such as a Crystal Pylon Base, which is used to setup a perimeter of protection to your claimed plot of land and manage your team relations. Additionally there are structures which act as machines, factories, turrets and crafting, which are all used to assist in automation of many tasks. Most structures require energy for usage, which may be generated by consuming fuels in a generator and/or stabilizing your power sources. The Crystal Pylon acts as your base structure, storing energy and providing energy to other structures on request. However; The Crystal is highly violate and vulnerable. It must be managed and stabilized via Up-keep, and protected from destruction by enemies or the protection offered will cease.

Scattered across the world are special regions that players may locate using a Prospecting tool. Within these regions one may build a structure that, when operated correctly, will 'mine' certain supported cryptocurrencies from the region into credit items in-game. This is accomplished via a low level pseudo Proof-Of-Work chain running within the game server.

The mining structures are used to house the hashing modules which are key in the mining of crypto from the world. The modules consume energy and run out after some time, but will mine crypto when inside a miner within a given resource node region. There are three additional types of modules (Speed, Efficiency, Productivity) which will modify the operation of some machines and factory structures. The Hashing Module may only be used within a Miner.

Included within CrystalCrypts is a Relational Teams based Factions control and permission management system. This is used to assist players in group organization, protection of local claimed land region and in handling of inter-player conflicts that may arise. Through the usage of Relationship statuses and grouped permission flags, the Teams/Relation feature set grants fine tuned control over what other players may do and perform when within your structures and territories. Each player has their own local team, which they are ranked as General (team owner) of. Every player is also able to become a member of or form an alliance to a team or another player. Given this, players can be within multiple teams at once and still remain control of their own local team. Team Generals are able to set a relation status with other players & teams, such as: Enemy, Neutral, Ally and more.. A team General can invite other players to join their team too, and assign them ranks such as Infantry, Cadet, and Officer. These relations and ranks are used to determine what actions one may or may not perform when within a territory or with a structure of another team.

Rewards

Rewards


Rewards is an economy plugin which allows players to earn daily rewards in the form of Credits. Credits can be spent in in game shops, to tip other players, pay for game entry and redeemed for crypto. Credits rewards are paid out every 24 hours to every player who has played at least 1 hour during that day at a rate of 20 Credits per day. Credits reward rate increases if you have a streak of consecutive days played. Once a 7 day streak is reached it resets.

Early peoples, curious at the sight of that majestic crystal quickly realized the power locked within and as early as technology allowed them they started extracting the mysterious energy within. Kingdoms quickly rose and fell and numerous wars were fought for the control of the precious crystal until one day, when the crystal exploded and shatered it's shards all across the known world.

Many ages later we still search for these shards which now hold only a fraction of it's original energy, yet still we thrive on the same mysterious energy that brought destruction on the ancient world. Years of experimentation taught people meddling in these things how to safely extract it's power and use it to power machinery big and small.
